I'm a Fullstack Web Developer based in the Orlando, Florida area. I graduated from New York University with a bachelor's degree in Economics. I worked in Manhattan as a Commercial Lines Insurance Broker at Marsh & McLennan before deciding to transition careers into web development. I recently graduated from University of Central Florida's Coding Boot Camp, learning Javascript and the MERN development stack. I am currently pursuing employment as a fullstack or back-end web developer. Blending my passion of programming with technical skills, I will deliver unique web development and design solutions.

1. Gamer

Ever since playing The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past with my father, I've been a lifelong fan of video games. I've recently been playing Counter-Strike: Global Offensive and League of Legends.

2. Headphones Collector

As a fan of all things music, I started researching into better headphones in college in pursuit of better sound quality. I now have a few mid-range open and closed-back headphones for different audio experiences.

3. Music Festival Goer

After going to my first Music Festival in 2014 in New York City, I've traveled all over the United States for various events, including Las Vegas, Atlanta, and San Bernardino. My next trip is in the summer of 2017 to Rothbury Michigan for Electric Forest.
